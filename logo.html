The Bulgarian Athletes Federation opened a branding contest, in which they invited creatives to redesign their brand identity. I joined with a design that aimed to bring a more contemporary feel, moving away from the formal logos usually associated with federations. By merging two elements into one, I created a dynamic -illustrative look that gave the federation an unique character.

Conclusion

Through each brand identity project, I’ve been able to sharpen my visual communication skills, through which i learn how to express the essence of a brand in new ways. It’s not just about improving the technical side, but also about blending creativity with strategy, and making identities that really stick with people.
