"Witchcraft Taboo" Publication

For a university collaborative assignment, my partner and I chose to explore the taboo of witchcraft, a topic considered highly sensitive in Bulgaria and Romania. We decided to present our research in the form of a traditional grimoire, but with a twist. The publication not only provides information on the subject but also draws the viewer into a more interactive experience. Through the use of glow-in-the-dark paint and star-shaped bookbinding, we were able to create a dynamic and engaging piece that sparks curiosity and invites audiences interaction.
