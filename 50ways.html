"50ways" artworks

In the cinema a race car was showered by potatoes. This is the sentence I came up with based on three words I got on a random principe -shower,cinema,race car. The purpose of this university assignment was to encourage exploration, experimentation, and creativity. In the spam of a month I had to create 50 illustrations with the same concept using different methods and techniques.

Research
During the research phase of the works, I spent time exploring the essential techniques and materials that I needed for creating the artworks. I dove into learneing as I went, and picking up ideas that would shape the way I approached every next illustration.
